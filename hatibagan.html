Hatibagan Branch

HATIBAGAN BRANCH

Generally, in the other outlets of arsalan no matter what day of the week or what time it is you simple ‘have to’ wait in the queue to get your table. However, the hatibagan outlet is larger than the others and it has a capacity of about 75+ seating.Hence the waiting is somewhat reduced.It is a 2 min walk from the town-school bus-stop.


Ambiance: The modern hanging lamps, the artwork on the walls give a very elegant feeling to the restaurant. The seating is spacious and comfortable.


Food: Commenting on arsalan food is like grilling about Shah Rukh Khan as an actor. It’s just versatile. The biriyani is one of the bests in kolkata. The butter chicken, chiken tikka masala , kebabs, nuns are all delicious.


Overall, the price is reasonable and the service is fast.This is a place that drags you back every time you think about north indian delicacies. An ideal place for a friends / colleagues meeting over lunch or a birthday treat or maybe a lunch date within budget. A must visit for biriyani lovers!
